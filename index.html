Meet Eunice


Passionate about women's transformation. For 10 years I worked as a hairstylist, believing that if women looked good on the outside, their lives would feel perfect.

But through thousands of conversations in the beauty industry, I discovered something deeper: true satisfaction doesn't come only from looking good.

It comes from how we see ourselves.

"The way you perceive yourself can either open unimaginable doors or limit you completely."

The Self-Image Discovery

I found one of the most powerful tools in this world: Self-Image. The only way to change your outside world is to reconnect with your inner self and build habits that align with your higher version.

Discover

Reconnect with your inner self and understand your true worth beyond external appearance.

Transform

Build daily habits that align with your higher version and authentic self-image.

Shine

Embrace your power to create, shine, and design the life you truly deserve.

Why I Created HERA?

Out of this vision, HERA Hair Essentials was born. More than products, it's a ritual: talking to yourself while looking in the mirror, embracing your worth, and caring for your hair with high-quality natural ingredients.

After 3 years walking this new journey, I feel ready to help women in many ways.
